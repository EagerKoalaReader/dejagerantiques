DE JAGER ANTIQUES

by Daniel Leirgulen


Daniel de Jager (yahguh) tracks, studies, and ultimately destroys occult texts that function as mechanisms for opening doors, binding minds, and leaving lasting wounds on those that encounter them. The series follows Daniel as both investigator and reluctant custodian of these texts. His work takes him across Europe and beyond, into abandoned churches, private collections, state archives, and charitable foundations. Fortnightly chapters.
