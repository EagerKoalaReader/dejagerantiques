DE JAGER ANTIQUES

by Daniel Leirgulen & J.D.Haff


Daniel de Jager (yahguh) tracks, studies, and ultimately destroys occult texts that function as mechanisms for opening doors, binding minds, and leaving lasting wounds on those that encounter them. The series follows Daniel as both investigator and reluctant custodian of these texts. His work takes him across Europe and beyond, into abandoned churches, private collections, and state archives. A small and unofficial network within the Vatican monitors the movement of these texts, providing Daniel with fragmentary records, sealed testimonies, and access to archives never meant to be opened again. Fortnightly chapters.
