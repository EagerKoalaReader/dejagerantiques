Privacy & Cookies

We care about privacy, and we don’t believe in collecting data just for the sake of it. This website exists to host a fictional web series, not to track people or build marketing profiles. That said, a few basic interactions on the site do involve other platforms, and this page explains what that means in clear terms.

What we Collect

We do not use analytics tools, tracking pixels, advertising cookies, or behavioural profiling of any kind. We don’t monitor how long you stay on the site, where you came from, or what else you browse. The only personal data we receive is information you actively choose to share with us. This can include:

• Your email address and any information you include if you contact us by email.

• Any information you choose to share publicly by leaving a comment through Facebook’s comment system.

• Any information you share when contacting us through linked social platforms.

• We don’t collect sensitive personal data, and we don’t attempt to identify anonymous visitors.

Emails & Direct Contact

If you email us, we’ll receive whatever information you include in that message, such as your email address and the content of your message. We use this information only to respond to you. We don’t add email addresses to mailing lists, sell them, or share them with third parties. Emails are kept only as long as reasonably necessary to deal with the conversation or enquiry.

Comments on the Site

Comments on the site are powered by Facebook’s comment system. This means that when you comment, Facebook may collect data according to their own privacy policies, not ours. We don’t control how Facebook processes that data, what cookies they use, or how long they retain information. If you’re concerned about this, you should review Facebook’s privacy settings and policies before commenting.

Social Media Links

The site includes links to a Threads account and a WhatsApp Business account, as well as sharing tools. Clicking these links takes you away from our website and onto platforms that operate under their own privacy rules. We don’t receive analytics or tracking data from these platforms about your visit to our site. Any interaction you have with them is governed by their respective privacy policies.

Cookies

This website does not set any cookies of its own. However, third-party services embedded or linked on the site, such as Facebook comments, may place cookies on your device. These cookies are controlled entirely by those third parties, not by us.

If you want to manage or block cookies, you can do so through your browser settings.

How to Protect Your Information

We take reasonable steps to protect any information you choose to share with us. That said, no method of transmission over the internet is ever completely secure, so we can’t guarantee absolute security. We don’t do anything risky with your data because, frankly, we don’t want it in the first place.

Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to:

• Ask what personal data we hold about you

• Request that we delete it

• Ask us to correct inaccurate information

If you want to do any of the above, just email us and we’ll deal with it sensibly and promptly.

Changes to This Policy

If the site changes in a way that affects privacy, for example, if we add new features that involve data collection, we’ll update this page accordingly.
