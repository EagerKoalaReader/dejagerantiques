What Personal Data We Collect

We do not collect personal data through user accounts, registrations, or marketing forms. However, limited data processing may occur in the following ways:

a) Local Storage (Saved Links Feature)

This website includes a “Save” feature that allows users to store links for later reading.

When this feature is used:

The page title and URL are stored locally in your browser using your browser’s local storage.

- This data remains solely on your device.

- It is not transmitted to us.

- It is not stored on any external server.

- We cannot access or retrieve this data.

If you clear your browser cookies, cache, or site data, your saved links will be permanently removed. This processing is based on your explicit action (Article 6(1)(a) GDPR; consent through interaction).

b) Server Log Data

Like most websites, the hosting provider may automatically log technical information such as:

- IP address

- Browser type

- Operating system

- Date and time of access

This information is used strictly for security, maintenance, and technical troubleshooting. We do not use this data for profiling or marketing. The legal basis for this processing is legitimate interest (Article 6(1)(f) GDPR), specifically ensuring website security and functionality.

Cookies

This website uses only essential cookies required for basic functionality.

We do not use:

- Advertising cookies

- Tracking cookies

- Third-party analytics scripts

- Behavioural profiling tools

If a cookie banner is displayed, it is for transparency and compliance purposes only. You may disable cookies through your browser settings at any time.

External Links and Third-Party Services

This website contains links to external platforms such as Raindrop.io, Goodlinks, and other sharing services.

When you click these links:

- Any data processing is governed by the privacy policy of the external platform.

Data Retention

We do not retain personal data through this website.

Any saved links stored using the “Save” feature remain in your browser until you manually delete them or clear your browser data. Server log data retention is determined by the hosting provider’s security policy.

- The right to access your personal data

Because this website does not collect or store personal data directly, most of these rights will not apply in practice. However, you may contact us if you have concerns. If you are in the UK, you may contact the Information Commissioner’s Office (ICO):
