De Jager Universe Lore

Possibility

Mathematics and physics describe how the universe already behaves, not why the rules responsible for that output exist in the first place. When an experiment is run precisely as it was the first time, the result is the same. If the rules governing the universe shifted randomly, cognition itself could not exist. Rules and rule-sets act like boundaries. They decide what can exist, in what state it exists, if it can move, and what outcomes are possible when interacting with other rules. Behind this structure is God the engineer.

Rule Structuring

Consider numbers. What is “one”? What makes it different from “two”? A number only means something because it is named, defined, and understood in relation to other things. Mathematics cannot exist without meaning and meaning cannot exist without language. The universe is built on such a language; the word. Semantic attribution assigned to individual operators that form rule. Should those individual operators (1) combine with others (2), a separate semantic attribution is assigned to the new paradigm. Rules are built on this basis, possibility rests on those rules. The wielder of the word can form new rules at will and we are subject to them. 

Consciousness Highways & Spectrum Lurking

Written into possibility, consciousness highways allow outside universe entities to place their accumulative experience (memories / personalities) into forms designed prior to entry. Whilst not able to change the rules / rule-sets, Angels are permitted to use these highways, typically to do God's bidding. Upon entry, their created forms are by default located outside the spectrum seen and heard by humans unless possessing keys (usually word combinations) or if they are invited into visible light. 

The Tapping

Unable to view thoughts or infiltrate minds directly, fallen angels are known to speak repeatedly the same phrase, whilst outside visible light, to persons in mental distress. This has the effect of generating images and ideas into a consciousness that would otherwise be ignored. This is strictly forbidden by God, even when attempting to achieve a benevolent outcome. Free will is strictly maintained.
