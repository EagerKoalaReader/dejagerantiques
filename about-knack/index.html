About Knack Publishing

Knack Publishing is a new independent studio that develops and owns original intellectual property, including the De Jager Universe. We build long-form stories first, then create physical works around them.

Our focus is simple: strong concepts, tight world-building, and controlled releases. Knack owns the IP it produces. That means we decide how each story expands, whether into limited edition comics, illustrated novels, board games, or collectable figurines. Every special edition item is produced in a fixed run of 100. No reprints and no extended waves. We also produce standalone limited editions not tied to our core IP. These follow the same model: small run, high attention to detail, no mass manufacturing. The physical editions are optional for those who want to own part of our world in a tangible form.

Knack operates differently from most digital publishers. We do not hide stories behind paywalls. We do not require forced sign-ups to read. We do not harvest unnecessary data. Readers can access our work freely and privately.

Our writing and special edition works are 100% AI assistance free, and writing is created entirely with standard spell-check functionality in various versions of everyday document creators. AI has no place in the creative arts. Please read our statement on the use of AI in the arts, here.
